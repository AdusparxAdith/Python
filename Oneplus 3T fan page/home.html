ONEPLUS 3T

The OnePlus 3T stays true to our Never Settle
approach.Impressive specs mean nothing if you
don’t have a great experience every time you
pick up your phone. The OnePlus 3T delivers
the best user experience,thanks to the latest
hardware upgrades & carefully tested software
enhancements. It was developed with care,
craftsmanship and in collaboration with
our community.
The OnePlus 3T challenges modern smartphone
aesthetics with its minimalist metal build
and bold Gunmetal color. Carefully engineered
using a premium space-grade aluminum alloy,
the OnePlus 3T has the resilience to go with
its sleek design.

The OnePlus 3T features the most refined
version of OxygenOS yet.It makes the most
of Android’s core functionalities while
adding a slew of customization options.The
result is an OS that is just as light and
customizable as stock Android,while building
on a couple of key areas to improve your
experience. We work with our community to
ensure every update is Perfect.
